## ChangePaceHelpSteady

steady - You travel about 8 hours a day, taking frequent rests. You
take care not to get too tired.

## ChangePaceHelpStrenuous

strenuous - You travel about 12 hours a day, starting just after sunrise
and stopping shortly before sunset. You stop to rest only when
necessary. You finish each day feeling very tired.

## ChangePaceHelpGrueling

grueling - You travel about 16 hours a day, starting before sunrise
and continuing until dark. You almost never stop to rest. You do not
get enough sleep at night. You finish each day feeling absolutely
exhausted, and your health suffers.
